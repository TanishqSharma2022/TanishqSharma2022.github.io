About Us

SAC is the student activity council of IISER Bhopal. This is the elected student body, which acts as a bridge between the administration and the students to ensure smooth functioning. We ensure that the concerns and demands of the students are properly conveyed to the administration. We are also responsible for all students activities hosted on campus We are not just about conducting fests and other activities inside the campus, we are also responsible for upholding the rights and values of the students.
