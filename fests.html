FESTS @iiserb

ENTHUZIA

Enthuzia is the annual socio-cultural fest conducted by the Indian Institute of Science, Education & Research, Bhopal. It is the most prominent fest organised in Central India. The prime focus of the fest is to guarantee enjoyment & learning opportunities through fun, exciting activities. The fest is a 3-day celebration of arts, culture & coming together of diverse communities. Exciting events & entertaining talent displays make Enthuzia a truly reveling experience for all attending audiences.

SINGULARITY

The annual science fest of IISER Bhopal. Singularity has expanded its entourage of influence, attracting college and school students alike to witness this extraordinary merry of science. Highlighting the promethean nuances of IISER as an institute meant to patronize, endow and give oxygen to scientific thinking, Singularity aims to promote out-of-the-box thinking and inculcate scientific ratiocination in the youth.

MUN

Model United Nations is an authentic simulation of the UN General Assembly, UN Security Council, or other multilateral bodies, which catapults participants into the world of diplomacy and negotiation. In Model UN, participants step into the shoes of ambassadors of UN member states, from Afghanistan to Zimbabwe to debate current issues on the Organisation's vast agenda.

IISM

The Inter IISER Sports Meet (abbreviated and better known as IISM) is the premier multi-sport event of the national institutes dedicated to basic sciences and research. Held annually in the month of December An event of its stature must represent the diverse sporting talent among its thousand odd participants. Accordingly, the tournament usually spans twelve broad sporting categories (Athletics, Badminton, Basketball, Chess, Cricket, Football, Kabaddi, Kho Kho, Lawn Tennis, Table Tennis, Carrom and Volleyball) with carrom being the latest addition.

IICM

IICM is the annual cultural festival and competition across the IISERs and other research institutions ( NISER, CEBS & IISc) conducted by CULTURAL COUNCIL.Several competitions of art, music, dance, theatre, filmmaking, literature, and photography take place spread over a span of three days.

A&L

Arts and Letters is the major fest conducted by the Fine Arts and Literary Council.

Armagaeddon

The annual e-gaming fest of IISER Bhopal. The Computing and Networking Council (CNC) promotes e-gaming and organises the Intra IISER e-gaming event, Armageddon.

Sangharsh

Sangharsh is the Intra IISERB Sports Tournament, held annually in the even semesters. It features most events spanning sports organised in the Inter IISER Sports Meets, apart from a few more. Most events are organised in batchwise teams. The tournament provides community members a chance to prove their prowess and have recreational fun, hence attracting large participation. It is also aimed at building the spirits of teamwork and sportsmanship, and promoting leadership qualities. Outstanding student performers and hidden talents are identified too here, and may get a chance to represent the institute in the IISM.
